Item #: SCP-006

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures:

Although SCP-006 poses no immediate danger, strict secrecy must be maintained. Personnel interacting with SCP-006 must be misinformed about its properties to prevent unauthorized use.

All personnel must wear modified Class VI BNC suits while in the vicinity of SCP-006. Any collected liquid is to be transported in a Quad-Sealant Container under armed guard.

Description:

SCP-006 is a small spring located near Astrakhan, Russia. The liquid emitted from the spring has been chemically identified as simple mineral water, but it possesses anomalous health properties.

Consumption of SCP-006 results in the repair of DNA damage, accelerated healing, and a highly improved immune response. Testing has shown that hostile bacteria and viruses are neutralized upon exposure to the liquid.
