Item #: SCP-002

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-002 must remain connected to a suitable power supply at all times. In case of total power failure, containment area must be sealed, and personnel must evacuate immediately.

Description:

SCP-002 appears to be a large, tumorous, fleshy growth with a volume of roughly 60 cubic meters. An entryway on one side leads to an interior that resembles a furnished apartment. However, all furniture and items inside are composed of bone, hair, and other biological material.

Testing has revealed that items found inside SCP-002 are, in fact, the remains of previous victims.

Discovery:

SCP-002 was discovered in a crater caused by an unknown impact event. It was recovered by the SCP Foundation and transported under heavy security.

Incident Report:

Several personnel went missing during early containment. Further investigation revealed that SCP-002 assimilates individuals who enter its interior, converting them into new pieces of furniture.
