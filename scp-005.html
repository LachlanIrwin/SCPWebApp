Item #: SCP-005

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-005 poses no immediate risk in any direct sense. However, due to its unique function, special measures are required to restrict access. Removal from its containment area requires approval from at least one Level 4 personnel.

Description:

SCP-005 is an ornate key resembling a typical mass-produced key from the 1920s. It was discovered when a civilian used it to infiltrate a high-security facility.

SCP-005 appears to have the ability to unlock any form of lock, mechanical or digital, with ease. The origin of this ability remains unknown.
