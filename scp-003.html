Item #: SCP-003

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-003 is to be maintained at a constant temperature of no less than 35°C and ideally kept above 100°C. No living multicellular organisms of Category IV or higher complexity may be allowed to come into contact with SCP-003.

In the event of total power failure, personnel must use direct skin contact to prevent SCP-003 from reaching activation temperature.

Description:

SCP-003 consists of two related components of separate origin, referred to as SCP-003-1 and SCP-003-2.

SCP-003-1 appears to be composed of biological material arranged similarly to a computer motherboard, predating modern circuit boards by thousands of years.

SCP-003-2, a stone tablet covered in unknown runes, appears to have been designed to contain SCP-003-1.

Addendums:

Further research suggests SCP-003-1 may be sentient. During an anomalous event, it briefly took the form of a humanoid entity before being re-contained.
